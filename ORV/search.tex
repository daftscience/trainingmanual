\newthought{Here are the options} and their descriptions:

\begin{description}
    \bolditem{Patient Identifier} Search for orders using a patient Identifier.
    \bolditem{Accession Number} Search for orders using an Accession Number. The orders displayed will be limited to those on the accession number.
    \bolditem{Date Range} When \textsc{Orders Between} is \faCheckSquareO 'd, orders will be limited to the date range set.
    \bolditem{Type of Activity} Select which types of orders are displayed. This is useful if too many unwanted orders are returned.
\end{description}
