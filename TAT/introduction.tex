The \textsc{TAT monitor} is used to view outstanding orders which have been received in the laboratory.

\section{How it Works}

Just like \textsc{Pending Inquiry}, the \textsc{TAT Monitor} is pulled using a {\sect}, {\subsect}, \bench, or \instrument. However, in addition, the \textsc{TAT Monitor} can be pulled using a \site.\sidenote{This will include all of the laboratories {\sects} and \subsects.}

\paragraph{Select} a \textsc{Service Resource} in the \textsc{Service Resource Field}.\\

\prettyimage{width=.8\textwidth}{graphics/service_resource}

\paragraph{Click} \btn{graphics/ok}\\

\prettyimage{width=\textwidth}{graphics/tat_pulled}

\section{What's Displayed}

The items displayed will be different for each Laboratory. Larger sites will only display \textsc{Stat} and possibly \textsc{Time Study} orders.

Smaller sites may display all orders.
