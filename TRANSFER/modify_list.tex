Transfer lists which have not been \textsc{Transfered} can be modified. This means containers can be added or removed from the list.


\section{Adding or Removing Containers}

\paragraph{Select} \boldcap{Not Transfered} from the \textsc{Transfer List} options.\\

\prettyimage{width=.5\textwidth}{graphics/transfered_option}

\paragraph{Select} the \boldcap{From Location}.

\paragraph{Select} the \boldcap{To Location}.

\paragraph{Update} the \boldcap{date}.

\paragraph{Click} \btn{graphics/retrieve}\\

\prettyimage{width=\textwidth}{graphics/list_view}

\paragraph{Select} the list to modify.

\paragraph{Click} \btn{graphics/modify}\\

\prettyimage{width=.8\textwidth}{graphics/modify_list}

\paragraph{Move} any containers to or from the list.

\infoblock{Removing an item from a transfer list will make it available to be put on another list.}

\paragraph{Click} \btn{graphics/update} when finished.

\paragraph{Select} \boldcap{Not Transfered} from the \textsc{Transfer List} options.\\

\paragraph{Select} the \boldcap{To Location}.
